### **Question 2: The Comparison**
```
What are other education foundations our size doing?

```

**What to show:**
- AI compares to peers
- Explains what "normal" looks like
- Notes if you're conservative/aggressive
- Gives context without judgment

---

### **Question 3: The FoundationMark Advantage**
```
How would our portfolio compare to other $30M education foundations? 
Where would we rank?
```

**What to show:**
- AI uses FoundationMark's 40,000 foundation database
- "You'd rank in the top 35% of similar foundations"
- "Most foundations your size allocate 55-65% to equities - you'd be at 60%"
- "This would likely earn a 4-star FoundationMark rating"
- Shows peer allocation comparison

---

### **Question 4: The Fear**
```
What if the stock market crashes? Will we still be able to give grants?
```

**What to show:**
- AI walks through worst case (2008 scenario)
- Shows recovery timeline
- Reassures with historical context
- Explains why diversification helps
